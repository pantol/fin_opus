## Evening 4 — Thursday 2026-07-16, 19:30 (sandbox)

**Settle:** Wednesday's pending order filled at the Thursday open:

| Fill | Qty | Price (PLN) | Fee (PLN) |
|---|---:|---:|---:|
| ENTER SPL | 1 | 676.35 | 3.00 |

The 3.00 PLN fee is the commission **minimum** kicking in: 0.38% of a
676 PLN order would be 2.57 PLN, so the floor applies — 0.44% effective.
Micro-orders forced by exposure caps are proportionally the most expensive
fills; the cost model captures that honestly.

**Mark-to-market:** equity **98 152.64 PLN** (−0.98% on the day), cash
5 103.04 PLN, 8 open positions. Benchmark: WIG20TR −0.20% (8 626.96 →
8 609.69) — the book underperformed on the day; banks and copper fell
harder than the index. Book path so far: 100 000.00 → 99 636.23 (Tue) →
99 124.09 (Wed) → 98 152.64 (Thu), −1.85% since inception vs WIG20TR −0.61%
over the same span (from the 2026-07-14 close). Three sessions carry no
statistical weight — which is exactly why the track record is meant to run
for months. Trailing stops: PKN moved up (136.86 → 137.76) on a green day
for Orlen; the rest held.

**Decide:** **zero new signals** — a first. Every instrument passing the
entry rules (close > SMA200 and positive 6-month momentum) is already in
the book, so the pending-order queue is empty going into Friday.
